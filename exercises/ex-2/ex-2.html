Lateralus

Black

then

white are

all I see

in my infancy

red and yellow then came to be

reaching out to me

lets me see

there is

so

much

more and

beckons me

to look through to these

infinite possibilities

as below so above and beyond I imagine

drawn outside the lines of reason

push the envelope

watch it bend


Lateralus was written and recorded by Tool in 2000.

The syllables of the lyrics shown follow the Fibonacci sequence .

1-1-2-3-5-8-5-3-2-1-1-2-3-5-8-13-8-5-3.

The time signature for the chorus changes from 9/8 to 8/8 to 7/8.

987 is the 16th number in the sequence.

Writen by Danny Carey, Justin Chancellor, Adam Jones, Maynard James Keenan of Tool
